ABOUT ACMIT AIR

THE BEST AUSTRALIAN DESIGNED AIR CLEANER

The ACMIT™ AIR has been specially designed to clean hidden air particles to free ourselves from the terrible air pollution, consequently making the air that we breath so much more fresh. With our lastest innovation in technology this is even able to clean the air so thoroughly that those with asthma and hay fever won't ever encounter a problem again.
