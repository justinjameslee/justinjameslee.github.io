Services

Goldline IT are professionals in network service and IT solutions. When you contact us, you won’t just get an answer,
you’ll get the full service, and we won’t leave until the job is complete.

Goldline IT

Disaster Recovery & Backup

Back up support is essential to any business operations. We implement systems to back up and store your work daily to ensure it is safe. We monitor the system and adjust over time to meet your changing needs. In case of emergency, we create a disaster recovery plan as well as provide our ongoing network support. We also provide offsite back up.

Security Content

Security in IT systems is of the utmost importance and when dealing with confidential information, we are your team. With such systems as firewalls and anti-virus software, we also provide email and web filtering. You can rest assured that your details and intellectual property is safe and protected. Contact us to identify your needs for secure software/hardware.

Computer & Printer Repair

We repair all types of computers including laptops. Goldline IT are skilled with all laptop brands and models, including Apple products. Our computer servicing ranges from virus removal to slow computers. On top of this we are also capable of providing an on-site printer repair service, ranging from printer setups to general troubleshooting. All completely fixed with utmost efficency so that your business can get back up to speed in no time.

IT Support & Maintenance

Whether we set up your IT systems or you just need maintenance and repairs, Goldline IT can work with you to provide an IT solution. We can be contacted by phone or online and remotely access your system to solve issues in the most efficient way possible and tailor a solution to you.

Does Goldline IT offer a free IT audit?

Yes we do, we offer a complimentary IT support audit for the companies in Melbourne area. Please contact us on 03-98168377 if you are interested.

Network Services

Whether it is getting two computers to talk, or several remote offices to synchronise, we can find the IT Solution for you. We provide network devices including routers, switches, VOIP hardware and software, servers, desktops and more. We will make your system work together efficiently to maximise overall performance.

Hosted Services

Goldline IT can help protect or organise your business with a variety of hosted services, providing filtering against spam and viruses for your emails or reliable, online calendars, schedules and emails services.

Mailguard provides a comprehensive range of Cloud based solutions addressing security and real-time risk intelligence for businesses. Mailguard constantly monitors and combats new threats in real-time while providing access to your emails via any web browser globally in a fully functioning, fully protected email environment, reducing the risk of lost opportunities. Goldline IT can help you set up your business with Mailguard to provide email protection, filtering and archiving without the need for you to worry about the technical side of things.

Microsoft Office 365 is the IT solution for your business. You can organise your e-mails, schedules, calendars and documents with ease anytime, anywhere – Office 365 is online 24/7 and is accessible on any computer and most handheld devices. Microsoft Office 365 also has the latest protection against spam and viruses, as well as a enterprise-grade reliability and disaster recovery guarantee to keep your data safe. At Goldline IT we can help you set up your business with Microsoft Office 365 so you can keep it organised and running efficiently while we manage your IT networks.

Internet Services
